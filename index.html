How to Play

  • Click on the icons to chose your move.
  • You can press button 'R' for Rock in the keyboard.
  • You can press button 'P' for Paper in the keyboard.
  • You can press button 'S' for Scissors in the keyboard.
  • You can toggle the button 'A' for autoPlay in the keyboard.
  • You can press the button 'Backspace' for Reset the Score in the keyboard.
